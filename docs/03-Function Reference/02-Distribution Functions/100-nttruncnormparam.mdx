# NTTRUNCNORMPARAM

Returns parameters of the triangular distribution for the specified support, mean and standard deviation.

## Syntax

```excel
NTTRUNCNORMPARAM(
    Min,
    Max,
    Mean,
    Stdev
)
```

### Parameters

- **_Min_** is a lower limit of a support of the distribution $a$.
- **_Max_** is a upper limit of a support of the distribution $b$.
- **_Mean_** is the arithmetic mean of the distribution.
- **_Stdev_** is the standard deviation of the distribution (Positive value).

## Remarks

- Restrictions of arguments:

$$
  a<b
$$
