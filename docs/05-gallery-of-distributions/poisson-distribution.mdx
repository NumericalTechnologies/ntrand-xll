# Poisson Distribution

## Where do you meet this distribution?

- Accident : The number of soldiers killed by horse-kicks each year in each corps
- [Queuing Theory](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Queueing_theory) : Number of phone calls per minute, number of access to web sever per minute
- Biology : Number of mutations
- Nuclear physics : the [nuclear decay](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_decay) of atoms
- [Risk management -- Operational risk](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operational_risk)

## Shape of Distribution

### Basic Properties

- A parameter $\nu$ is required.

$$
  \nu>0
$$

$\nu$ is [mean](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_mean) of the distribution.

- Discrete distribution defined at non-negative integer $x={0,1,2,\cdots}$

### Probability

- [Cumulative distribution function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_cumulative)

  $$
  F(x)=\text{e}^{-\nu}\sum_{i=0}^{x}\frac{\nu^i}{i !}
  $$

- [Probability mass function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_probabilitymass)

  $$
  f(x)=\frac{\nu^x\text{e}^{-\nu}}{x !}
  $$

## Characteristics

### Mean -- Where is the "center" of the distribution? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_mean))

- Mean is given as $\nu$

### Standard Deviation -- How wide does the distribution spread? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation))

- Standard deviation is given as $\nu$

  [Standard Deviation](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation) is a positive square root of [Variance](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_variance).

### Skewness -- Which side is the distribution distorted into? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_skewness))

- [Skewness](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_skew) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  \frac{1}{\sqrt{\nu}}
  $$

### Kurtosis -- Sharp or Dull, consequently Fat Tail or Thin Tail ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_kurtosis))

- [Kurtosis](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_kurtosis) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  \frac{1}{\nu}
  $$

## NtRand Functions

- If you already have parameters of the distribution
  - Generating random numbers based on Mersenne Twister algorithm: [NTRANDPOISSON](https://www.ntrand.com/ntrandpoisson/)
  - Computing probability : [NTPOISSONDIST](https://www.ntrand.com/ntpoissondist/)
  - Computing mean : [NTPOISSONMEAN](https://www.ntrand.com/ntpoissonmean/)
  - Computing standard deviation : [NTPOISSONSTDEV](https://www.ntrand.com/ntpoissonstdev/)
  - Computing skewness : [NTPOISSONSKEW](https://www.ntrand.com/ntpoissonskew/)
  - Computing kurtosis : [NTPOISSONKURT](https://www.ntrand.com/ntpoissonkurt/)
  - Computing moments above at once : [NTPOISSONMOM](https://www.ntrand.com/ntpoissonmom/)
