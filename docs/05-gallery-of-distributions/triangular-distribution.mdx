# Triangular Distribution

## Where do you meet this distribution?

- [Project management](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_management) -- [PERT](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PERT), [CPM](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Critical_path_method) and so on
- Digital signal processing ([dithering](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dither)) -- digital audio, digital video, digital photography, seismology, RADAR, weather forecasting systems and many more
- Data security

  ["Truncated Triangular Distribution for Multiplicative Noise and Domain Estimation" by Jay J. Kim and Dong M. Jeong](http://www.amstat.org/sections/srms/Proceedings/y2008/Files/300855.pdf)

- [Business simulation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triangular_distribution#Business_simulations) (Corporate finance)
- Proxy of [Beta distribution](https://www.ntrand.com/beta-distribution/)

## Shape of Distribution

### Basic Properties

- Three parameters $a, b, c$ are required ([How can you get these](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularparam/)).

  $$
  a<c<b
  $$

  These parameters are minimum value of variable, maximum value of variable and [mode](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_mode) of the distribution respectively.

- Continuous distribution defined on bounded range $a\leq x \leq b$
- This distribution can be symmetric or asymmetric.

### Probability

- [Cumulative distribution function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_cumulative)

  $$
  F(x)=\begin{cases}\frac{(x-a)^2}{(b-a)(c-a)}\quad&(a\leq x<c)\\1-\frac{(b-x)^2}{(b-a)(b-c)}\quad&(c\leq x\leq b)\end{cases}
  $$

- [Probability density function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_probability)

  $$
  f(x)=\begin{cases}\frac{2(x-a)}{(b-a)(c-a)}\quad&(a\leq x<c)\\\frac{2(b-x)}{(b-a)(b-c)}\quad&(c\leq x\leq b)\end{cases}
  $$

### Quantile

- Inverse function of [cumulative distribution function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_cumulative)

  $$
  F^{-1}(P)=\begin{cases}\sqrt{P(c-a)(b-a)}+a\quad&\left(P< \frac{c-a}{b-a}\right)\\-\sqrt{(1-P)(b-c)(b-a)}+b\quad&\left(P\geq \frac{c-a}{b-a}\right)\end{cases}
  $$

### Standard Deviation -- How wide does the distribution spread? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation))

- [Variance](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_variance) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  \frac{a^2+b^2+c^2-ab-bc-ca}{18}
  $$

  [Standard Deviation](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation) is a positive square root of [Variance](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_variance).

### Skewness -- Which side is the distribution distorted into? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_skewness))

- [Skewness](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_skewness) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  \frac{\sqrt{2}(a+b-2c)(2a-b-c)(a-2b+c)}{5(a^2+b^2+c^2-ab-bc-ca)^{3/2}}
  $$

### Kurtosis -- Sharp or Dull, consequently Fat Tail or Thin Tail ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_kurtosis))

- Kurtosis is $-0.6$ .

## Random Numbers

- Random number x is generated by inverse function method, which is for uniform random U,

  $$
  x=\begin{cases}\sqrt{U(c-a)(b-a)}+a\quad&\left(U< \frac{c-a}{b-a}\right)\\-\sqrt{(1-U)(b-c)(b-a)}+b\quad&\left(U\geq \frac{c-a}{b-a}\right)\end{cases}
  $$

## NtRand Functions

- If you already have parameters of the distribution
  - Generating random numbers based on Mersenne Twister algorithm: [NTRANDTRIANGULAR](https://www.ntrand.com/ntrandtriangular/)
  - Computing probability : [NTTRIANGULARDIST](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangulardist/)
  - Computing quantile : [NTTRIANGULARINV](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularinv/)
  - Computing mean : [NTTRIANGULARMEAN](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularmean/)
  - Computing standard deviation : [NTTRIANGULARSTDEV](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularskew/)
  - Computing skewness : [NTTRIANGULARSKEW](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularskew/)
  - Computing kurtosis : [NTTRIANGULARKURT](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularkurt/)
  - Computing moments above at once : [NTTRIANGULARMOM](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularmom/)

  - Estimating parameters of the distribution:[NTTRIANGULARPARAM](https://www.ntrand.com/nttriangularparam/)
