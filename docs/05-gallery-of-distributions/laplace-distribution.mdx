# Laplace Distribution

## Where will you meet this distribution?

- Marketing

  ["On the Laplace Distribution of Firms Growth Rates" by Giulio Bottazzi and Angelo Secchi](http://ideas.repec.org/p/ssa/lemwps/2002-20.html)

- Computer graphics

  ["State assignment of the JPEG statistical model based on a Laplacian distribution" by KUROKI Yoshimitsu el al](http://ci.nii.ac.jp/naid/110003706110/)

## Shape of Distribution

### Basic Properties

- Two parameters $\mu, \phi$ are required.

  $$
  \phi>0
  $$

- Continuous distribution defined on entire range
- This distribution is always symmetric.

### Probability

- [Cumulative distribution function](/docs/glossary#cumulative-distribution-function)

  $$
  F(x)=\begin{cases}\frac{1}{2}\exp\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\phi}\right)\;&(x<\mu)\\1-\frac{1}{2}\exp\left(-\frac{x-\mu}{\phi}\right)\;&(x\geq \mu)\end{cases}
  $$

- [Probability density function](/docs/glossary#probability-density-function)

  $$
  f(x)=\frac{1}{2\phi}\exp\left(-\frac{|x-\mu|}{\phi}\right)
  $$

### Quantile

- Inverse function of [cumulative distribution function](/docs/glossary#cumulative-distribution-function)

  $$
  F^{-1}(P)=\begin{cases}\phi\ln 2P+\mu&(P<0.5)\\-(\phi\ln 2(1-P)+\mu)&(P\geq 0.5)\end{cases}
  $$

## Characteristics

### Mean -- Where is the "center" of the distribution? ([Definition](/docs/glossary#mean))

- Mean of the distribution is given as $\mu$.

### Standard Deviation -- How wide does the distribution spread? ([Definition](/docs/glossary#standard-deviation))

- [Variance](/docs/glossary#variance) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  2\phi^2
  $$

  [Standard Deviation](/docs/glossary#standard-deviation) is a positive square root of [Variance](/docs/glossary#variance).

- How to compute this on Excel

### Skewness -- Which side is the distribution distorted into? ([Definition](/docs/glossary#skewness))

- Skewness of the distribution is $0$.

### Kurtosis -- Sharp or Dull, consequently Fat Tail or Thin Tail ([Definition](/docs/glossary#kurtosis))

- Kurtosis of the distribution is $3$.

## Random Numbers

- Random number x is generated by inverse function method, which is for uniform random U,

  $$
  x=\begin{cases}\phi\ln 2U+\mu&(U<0.5)\\-(\phi\ln 2(1-U)+\mu)&(U\geq 0.5)\end{cases}
  $$
