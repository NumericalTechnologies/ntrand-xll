# Chi Distribution

## Shape of Distribution

### Basic Properties

- One parameter $N$ is required (Positive integer)
- Continuous distribution defined on semi-bounded range $x \geq 0$
- This distribution is asymmetric.

### Probability

- [Probability density function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_probability)

  $$
  f(x)=\frac{1}{2^{\frac{N}{2}-1}\Gamma\left(\frac{N}{2}\right)}\exp\left(-\frac{x^2}{2}\right)x^{N-1}
  $$

  , where $\Gamma(\cdot)$ is [gamma function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_Gamma).

- [Cumulative distribution function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_cumulative)

  $$
  F(x)=\frac{\Gamma_{\frac{x^2}{2}}\left(\frac{N}{2}\right)}{\Gamma\left(\frac{N}{2}\right)}
  $$

  , where $\Gamma_{x}(\cdot)$ is [incomplete gamma function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_incomplete_gamma).

## Characteristics

### Mean -- Where is the "center" of the distribution? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_mean))

- [Mean](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_mean) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  \sqrt{2}\frac{\Gamma\left(\frac{N+1}{2}\right)}{\Gamma\left(\frac{N}{2}\right)}
  $$

  , where $\Gamma(\cdot)$ is [gamma function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_Gamma).

### Standard Deviation -- How wide does the distribution spread? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation))

- [Variance](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_variance) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  N-2\left[\frac{\Gamma\left(\frac{N+1}{2}\right)}{\Gamma\left(\frac{N}{2}\right)}\right]^2
  $$

  [Standard Deviation](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation) is a positive square root of [Variance](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_variance).

### Skewness -- Which side is the distribution distorted into? ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_skewness))

- [Skewness](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_skew) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  \frac{\mu(3)-3\mu(2)\mu(1)+2\mu^3(1)}{\sigma^3}
  $$

  $$
  \mu(r)=\frac{2^{\frac{r}{2}}\Gamma\left(\frac{N+r}{2}\right)}{\Gamma\left(\frac{N}{2}\right)}
  $$

  , where $\sigma$ is [standard deviation](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation) and $\Gamma(\cdot)$ is [gamma function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_Gamma).

### Kurtosis -- Sharp or Dull, consequently Fat Tail or Thin Tail ([Definition](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_kurtosis))

- [Kurtosis](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_kurtosis) of the distribution is given as

  $$
  \frac{\mu(4)-4\mu(3)\mu(1)+6\mu(2)\mu^2(1)-3\mu^4(1)}{\sigma^4}-3
  $$

  $$
  \mu(r)=\frac{2^{\frac{r}{2}}\Gamma\left(\frac{N+r}{2}\right)}{\Gamma\left(\frac{N}{2}\right)}
  $$

  , where $\sigma$ is [standard deviation and $\Gamma(\cdot)$ is ](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_standard_deviation)[gamma function](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_Gamma).

- This distribution can be [leptokurtic](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_leptokurtic) or [platykurtic](https://www.ntrand.com/glossary/#local_platykurtic).

## NtRand Functions

- If you already have parameters of the distribution
  - Generating random numbers based on Mersenne Twister algorithm: [NTRANDCHI](https://www.ntrand.com/ntrandchi/)
  - Computing probability : [NTCHIDIST](https://www.ntrand.com/ntchidist/)
  - Computing mean : [NTCHIMEAN](https://www.ntrand.com/ntchimean/)
  - Computing standard deviation : [NTCHISTDEV](https://www.ntrand.com/ntchiskew/)
  - Computing skewness : [NTCHISKEW](https://www.ntrand.com/ntchiskew/)
  - Computing kurtosis : [NTCHIKURT](https://www.ntrand.com/ntchikurt/)
  - Computing moments above at once : [NTCHIMOM](https://www.ntrand.com/ntchimom/)
